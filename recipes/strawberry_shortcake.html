Strawberry Shortcake

Description

Strawberry shortcake consists of a sweet biscuit with a that is served with chopped strawberries in a sauce and with whipped cream. It's rich, creamy, and crumbly--a great combination of textures.

Ingredients

  • 2 pints strawberries
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 cups flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/4 cups butter
  • 3 cups whipping cream
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Hull and dice strawberries. Gently crush some of them to release juices, then mix them with the 1/2 cup of sugar.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  3. Mix together the flour, 3 tbsp of sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Add 3/4 cup of butter, then 1 1/4 cups cream, then mix into dough.
  5. Knead the dough, then roll it out to a 1/2-inch thickness. Use a biscuit cutter to cut at rounds (biscuits).
  6. Use a dab of butter to lightly grease a baking sheet and the rounds. Bake for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven, then pull shortcakes apart. Apply some butter again.
  8. Beat the cream, add vanilla, then beat again.
  9. Assemble shortcakes, strawberries, and whipped cream however you like!
