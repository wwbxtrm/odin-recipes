Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef stroganoff is a dish of Russian origin, using saut√©ed pieces of beef served in a mustard sauce and sour cream over pasta, often with mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 1.2 lb boneless rib eye
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 10 oz mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8-10 oz pasta or noodles

Instructions

  1. Flatten steaks and trim fat from them.
  2. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Briefly heat half of the beef in oil over high heat. Brown for about 30 seconds.
  4. Remove, and then repeat with other half of beef, adding oil.
  5. Lower to medium heat, add butter. Add onions, cook for 1 minute, then add mushrooms.
  6. Lightly brown mushrooms, scraping bottom of skillet.
  7. Add flour, cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  8. Add half the broth while stirring. After a bit, add other half.
  9. Stir, adding sour cream and mustard.
  10. Bring to simmer, then lower heat to medium low. After 3-5 minutes, add salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Reintroduce beef. Simmer for 1 minute.
  12. Remove from stove, then serve over pasta.
